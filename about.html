EGYPLAST History

Est 1996

Our Story

Egyptian Company for Plastic Industry ELSEWEDY EGYPLAST was established in 1996 as a subsidiary of ELSEWEDY ELECTRIC. At EGYPLAST we create polymers compounding and processing for a sustainable future. We combine economic success with environmental protection and social responsibility. The approximately 800 employees work on contributing to the success of our customers in various sectors locally and worldwide. Our portfolio is organized into five segments: PVC Compound, Masterbatch, Special Compounds, PP Fibers and Fiberglass Poles. EGYPLAST considered as one of the largest Manufacturers of Plastic Compounds and Masterbatch in the Middle East and Africa with annual production capacity 140000 Ton.

In the light of our growth strategy, Two Factories have been added to diversify our product range; the first is the Fiberglass Poles Factory, which was established in 1998 by German Technology, equipped with two production lines of annual production capacity of 30000 Poles, Covering Street lighting, Decorative & landscape applications. And the Second is PP Fibers factory, which was established in 1998 by German Technology, equipped with three production lines and annual production capacity reaches 17000 tons.

About a group of success partners

Holding Company for Drinking Water and Wastewater

NOPWASD

The Holding Company for Drinking Water and Wastewater was established by Presidential Decree No. 135 of 2004 establishing a holding company for drinking water and wastewater and its subsidiaries. It shall have a legal personality and be subject to the provisions of Law No. 203 of 1991 and its executive regulations.

UNIONAIRE

high-tech home furnishings company

Unionaire is a well-established, high-tech home furnishings company that caters to the desires and needs of every home. Founded in Egypt in 1995, it was founded with a vision and commitment to providing customers with high-quality home furnishing products both locally and internationally.

United Carton Industries Company

UCIC

United Carton Industries Company focuses on high quality corrugated solutions for customized packaging needs, to meet our customer's requirements. UCIC has five corrugated state-of-the-art plants producing a variety of products including regular slotted or die-cut cartons, shelf-ready, high graphic prints, corrugated stands, and digital printed boxes.

packaging Materials

electrical products
